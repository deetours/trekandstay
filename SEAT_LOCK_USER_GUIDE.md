## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to manually "lock" the seats?
**A:** No! Just click the trip card. Seats lock automatically. âœ“

### Q: When does the lock start?
**A:** Immediately when you click the trip card in Step 1.

### Q: How long do I have?
**A:** 10 minutes (600 seconds) from when you click the trip card.

### Q: What if I take more than 10 minutes?
**A:** That's OK! You'll see an error message. Just select the trip again to get a new 10-minute lock.

### Q: Can I change the number of travelers?
**A:** Yes! If you change from 1 to 2+ travelers in Step 2, the system re-locks with the new count.

### Q: What if the trip is fully booked?
**A:** You'll see: "âš ï¸ Sorry, these seats are no longer available"  
Try selecting fewer travelers or choose a different trip.

### Q: Is my payment locked in too?
**A:** No, only the seats. Payment is separate once you confirm details.

### Q: Can I go back to edit details?
**A:** Yes! Click "Back to Details" button in payment step. Timer keeps counting (that's OK).

### Q: What if I close the modal?
**A:** Lock expires after 10 minutes regardless. Just open modal again and select trip to get a fresh lock.
